Known Limitations by OS

Known limitations by operating system

Fedora 23

  • trendmain and trendreport are not able to launch web browser to open -report.html automatically

OS X 10.7 to 10.8

  • GIF movie option of trendreconstruct is not supported

OS X 10.11

  • The strict safety precautions of El Capitan prevent double clicking or use of the open command in terminal from launching TREND apps. The TREND apps may nonetheless be launched in a terminal (text) window. To enable opening of the apps by double clicking, enter the system preferences menu and go to Security & Privacy and select Allow app downloaded from anywhere, at least temporarily until you have run each of the apps.
